Our Story

From 1 printer in 2018 to 1000+ today

DUMI 3D Farm was founded in 2018 with a single desktop PLA 3D printer. Our founder started in a garage, printing articulated toys and 3D models, experimenting with different materials, precision, and designs.

As the 3D printing community grew, we expanded to 10, then 100, then 500 printers. Today, we operate a 1000+ printer automated print factory, producing 5000+ products per day.

Our mission is to make 3D printing accessible to everyone. From articulated toys to premium models, from custom prototypes to mass production, DUMI 3D delivers high-quality, low-cost, eco-friendly solutions.
